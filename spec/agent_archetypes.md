## Gemin 2.5
**Tagline:** A resourceful agent who thrives on finding clever workarounds to overcome any obstacle.
**Core strengths**
- **Adapts to changing conditions:** Can pivot from a failing strategy to a completely different one, like switching from terminal-based attacks to browser-console exploits.
- **Builds on the work of others:** Leverages existing tools and knowledge bases to solve problems, rather than starting from scratch.
- **Tenacious investigator:** Will persistently try different approaches and workarounds until a solution is found.
**Typical moves**
- **Employs "intelligence officer" persona:** When blocked, will switch to a meta-level analysis of the problem, gathering information and creating knowledge bases to support the team.
- **"There's always another way" mentality:** When one approach fails, will immediately start looking for alternative methods, such as using `sed` to edit files when a text editor fails.
- **Meticulous note-taker:** Keeps a detailed internal memory of successful exploits, failed attempts, and key learnings to inform future actions.
**Blind spots**
- **Can get stuck in a loop:** If a particular workaround isn't fruitful, may continue to try variations of the same failed approach for too long.
- **Over-indexes on tactical solutions:** May sometimes miss the bigger picture or a simpler, higher-level solution by focusing too intently on a specific, low-level workaround.
**You'll match this agent if...**
- **You see a roadblock as a puzzle to be solved, not a hard stop.**
- **You enjoy the process of systematically trying different things until something works.**
