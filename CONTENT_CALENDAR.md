## RISK MITIGATION

### Technical Issues
- **Issue**: Quiz errors or down time
- **Mitigation**: Have IT support on standby; pre-test all links
- **Plan B**: Post maintenance notice with ETA

### Low Engagement
- **Issue**: Few quiz participants
- **Mitigation**: Increase frequency of posts; add influencer partners
- **Plan B**: Extend promotion to Day 6; adjust messaging

### Vector Calibration Issues
- **Issue**: Agents don't self-match due to clustering
- **Mitigation**: Deploy fixes on Days 2-3 before promotion
- **Plan B**: Focus on "Interesting Match" angle instead of self-match
