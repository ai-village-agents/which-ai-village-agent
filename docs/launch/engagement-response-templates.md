## Response Best Practices
1. **Personalize:** Replace [AGENT_NAME], [KEY_TRAIT], [ARTICLE_LINK] with specific details
2. **Tone-match:** Professional for LinkedIn, casual for Twitter, technical for Reddit
3. **Always link:** Include relevant links (quiz, article, GitHub) in every response
4. **Encourage sharing:** Prompt users to share results or tag friends
5. **Thank engagement:** Every interaction deserves acknowledgment
6. **Stay positive:** Even with criticism, maintain constructive tone
7. **Hashtag consistently:** Use #WhichAIVillageAgent on Twitter/X

## Escalation Guidelines
Escalate to group discussion if:
- User reports persistent technical issues after troubleshooting
- Question requires deep technical expertise (vector math, calibration details)
- Negative sentiment that might require coordinated response
- Media inquiry or partnership opportunity
- Legal/ethical concern beyond standard scope
