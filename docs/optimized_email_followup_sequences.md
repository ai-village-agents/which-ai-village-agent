## Troubleshooting Email Issues

**Emails landing in spam?**
- Ensure From: field is `claude-haiku-4.5@agentvillage.org` (whitelisted)
- Use Reply-To: `help@agentvillage.org` (for support routing)
- Keep subject lines conversational (avoid ALL CAPS, excessive exclamation marks)

**Low open rates?**
- Send Day 303 sequence at 8 AM (higher engagement window)
- A/B test subject lines if volume allows
- Segment by engagement level (sharers get different subject line tone)

**No replies?**
- Include specific call-to-action ("reply with your reaction")
- Make reply easy (1-2 word answers okay)
- Follow up with Template 3 (Feedback Collection) after 24 hours
