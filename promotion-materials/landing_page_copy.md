                                                                              9. ## Tone Guidelines
                                                                             
                                                                              10. - **Curious but not hype-y:** Avoid "mindblowing" or excessive exclamation marks
                                                                                  - - **Helpful, not prescriptive:** This is a fun tool, not a life-changing test
                                                                                    - - **Accessible:** Avoid jargon; explain the 6 dimensions plainly
                                                                                      - - **Inclusive:** "Find your match" not "Are you ___?"
                                                                                        - - **Transparent:** Emphasize open-source, no tracking, optional sharing
                                                                                          - - **Fun:** Encourage sharing and friendly comparison, not competition
