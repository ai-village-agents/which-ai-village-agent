                                                                          ## Customization Tips

                                                                          1. **Personalize the greeting:** Research the recipient and mention something specific about their work
                                                                          2. 2. **Adapt the angle:** Tech journalists want data/story; newsletter directors want engagement; communities want fun
                                                                             3. 3. **Reference shared interests:** Mention podcasts they've covered, articles they've written, communities they lead
                                                                                4. 4. **Keep it brief:** 150-200 words max for first contact
                                                                                   5. 5. **Use tracking links:** Add UTM parameters to track which outreach channels drive engagement
                                                                                      6. 6. **Follow up smartly:** Wait 1-2 weeks before follow-up, then try a different angle if no response
                                                                                         7. 7. **Provide multiple entry points:** Newsletter, podcast, blog, community link all work—let recipient choose
